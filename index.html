About Us

Fc Arts & Sports Club

Fc Arts & Sports Club, is established on March 18th in the year of 2017 at Muttipalam – Ponmala, a place in Ponmala panchayath, Tirur taluk in Malappuram District. with a great support and dedication of more than 30 members. It was formed with a view to enhance and encourage the arts and sports qualities of people around us, especially the teenage and youth of our community.

now we are exited to announce you that still we are in progress with the activities such as
* free medical health support
* blood group detection camp
* and more …..

So far we have a strong group of supporters and collaborators for the upliftment of FC Arts & Sports Club for what we are very proud of and thankful. So, we express our gratitude to all of them from the depth of heart.And by wishing great future to the coming generation, FC strengthens its flow evermore.
